"I am an AI Engineer and Researcher"

My work and research focus on various domains of Artificial Intelligence, particularly GenAI and multimodal learning. My expertise extends to computer vision, and I am currently working on AI applications in healthcare. The goal of my research is to use GenAI to train foundation models for generating synthetic data and providing insightful health analysis.

I am also passionate about AI model explainability and the ethical implications of AI, striving to ensure that AI systems are transparent, understandable, and deployed with a focus on fairness, accountability, and transparency.
